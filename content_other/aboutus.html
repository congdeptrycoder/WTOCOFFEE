    OUR PROCESS

    Every Coffee Bean are sourced from coffee-specialized hills in the highlands of Western Highlands, Vietnam.

    With the desire to provide customers with a pure coffee experience, awakening the most authentic flavors of coffee, all our coffee beans are harvested at a 95% ripeness rate and hand-picked in batches

    After processing, the final product will be green coffee beans. We will deliver sacks of coffee beans to you, shipping worldwide—just place your order!

    We ship by sea, so if you would like to place an order, please submit your information in the ORDER section. We will provide you with reputable certification documents, let you inspect the final product, or you can visit us in person to experience our coffee before placing an order.

    ABOUT US

    I am deeply passionate about the flavor of coffee. On one fortunate occasion, I met Mr. Luong Van Tu - Former Deputy Minister of Industry and Trade, Head of the WTO Agreement Negotiation Delegation, and Former Chairman of the Vietnam Coffee-Cocoa Association. With his advisory support, I decided to establish the WTO COFFEE brand with the goal of promoting Vietnamese coffee to a wider audience. WTO COFFEE offers premium green coffee beans (Robusta and Arabica) with a ripening rate of up to 95%, guaranteed to satisfy even the most discerning customers

    THE ORIGIN OF COFFEE BEANS

    Our green coffee beans are sourced from the highlands of the Central Highlands, at elevations of over 1000 meters above sea level. They are carefully processed by the skilled hands of experienced farmers, and every product is inspected by our advisor before being delivered to you. If you have the opportunity, we invite you to visit Vietnam, where we will take you to see our incredibly majestic coffee plantations in person.
